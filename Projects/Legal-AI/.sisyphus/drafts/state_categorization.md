# State Categorization Strategy

## Overview

**Purpose**: Define priority tiers for phased rollout of OpenStates crawler extension across 52 jurisdictions

**Rationale**: Prioritize by population size, legal activity, and data volume to maximize impact per unit effort

---

## Priority Tiers

### Tier 1: CRITICAL (6 states, 35% population)

**States**: CA, FL, NY, TX, IL, PA

**Selection Criteria**:
- Large population (> 12% each)
- High legislative volume (> 10,000 bills/year)
- Major legal markets
- Strong LLM training value

**Timeline**: Week 1-2 (Days 1-10)

**Success Metrics**:
- 6 jurisdictions covered
- ~6,000 bills collected
- 35% population coverage

---

### Tier 2: CRITICAL (6 states, 18% population)

**States**: OH, MI, GA, NC, NJ, VA, WA

**Selection Criteria**:
- Medium-large population (2-5% each)
- Significant legislative activity
- Industrial/regulatory focus

**Timeline**: Week 2-3 (Days 11-21)

**Success Metrics**:
- 6 jurisdictions covered
- ~5,000 bills collected
- 53% total population coverage

---

### Tier 3: HIGH (6 states, 12% population)

**States**: AZ, MA, IN, MO, MD, WI

**Selection Criteria**:
- Medium population (1-2% each)
- Moderate legislative volume
- Regional legal hubs

**Timeline**: Week 3-4 (Days 22-28)

**Success Metrics**:
- 6 jurisdictions covered
- ~3,000 bills collected
- 65% total population coverage

---

## Total Implementation Timeline

- **Week 1-2**: Tier 1-2 (12 states)
- **Week 3**: Tier 3 (6 states) - verification
- **Week 4**: Tier 4-5 (12 states)
- **Week 5**: Tier 6-7 (12 states)
- **Week 6**: Tier 8 (4-6 states)
- **Week 7**: Remaining states + cleanup
